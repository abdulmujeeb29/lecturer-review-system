About Us

We are a team of developers who have created the Lecturer Review System to make it easy for students to provide feedback on their lecturers. Our goal is to improve the learning experience for everyone by gathering valuable information and facilitating positive change in the classroom.

Why Provide Feedback? Feedback is essential to improving any system, and the education system is no exception. By providing feedback on your lecturers, you can help them understand what they're doing well and what areas they could improve upon. This benefits not only the lecturers themselves, but also future students who will learn from them..
